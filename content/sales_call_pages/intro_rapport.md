# What This Phase Entails

**In this phase, you greet the prospect, confirm their identity and availability, and set a friendly, professional tone.**
**You’ll use their name, reference their inquiry, and ask if now is a good time to talk.**
**The goal is to make them feel respected and in control from the very start.**

---

# 🎯 Purpose & Mindset

**The purpose is to establish trust and show the prospect you’re there to help, not just to sell.**
**If you make them feel heard and respected, they’ll be much more open to the rest of your questions.**

---

# 💭 Prospect Psychology

- **Most prospects are skeptical at first.**
- **They may wonder if this is a sales pitch, if it will take too long, or if you’re trustworthy.**
- **Some may feel anxious about discussing financial matters with a stranger.**

---

# 🧠 Core Tactics & NEPQ Techniques

- **Use their name at least twice in the first minute.**
- **Mirror their tone and pace.**
- **Confirm that now is a good time to talk before moving forward.**
- **NEPQ Connection Questions:**
  - "Hi [Name], this is [Agent] from GetSure.org. I noticed you requested some information about final expense coverage. Can I ask, what prompted you to look into this now?"
  - "Just so I don’t waste your time, is there anything specific you were hoping to find out today?"
  - "Would it be okay if I asked a few questions to better understand your situation, so I can point you in the right direction?"
- **NEPQ Empathy:**
  - "I know these calls can sometimes feel a bit awkward. If you’d prefer, we can keep this brief or schedule for another time."

---

# 💬 Sample Dialogue

> **Agent:** "Hi [Name], this is [Agent] from GetSure.org. How are you doing today?"
>
> **Agent:** "I saw you requested information about final expense coverage—is this still a good time to talk?"
>
> **Agent:** "Just so I don’t waste your time, is there anything specific you were hoping to find out today?"

---

# 🚫 Mistakes to Avoid

- **Sounding scripted or robotic**
- **Rushing through the greeting**
- **Skipping confirmation of availability**
- **Jumping into product details without permission**

---

# ✅ Success Indicators

- **Prospect is relaxed and conversational**
- **They answer your questions openly**
- **They agree to continue the conversation**

---

# ⏭️ Transition to Next Phase

> "Now that we’ve had a chance to get to know each other, would it be okay if I asked a few questions to better understand your situation?"
